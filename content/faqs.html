Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Frequently Asked Questions, and some background information.

What do the hedgehogs (and mice) eat? Through the summer we were originally giving them Spike's Hedgehog Food. However, lots was being wasted and going off quickly. We switched to cat and dog biscuits when the Autumn came, of which we have now settled on Go Cat

Where have the hedgehogs gone? If you are looking for footage from the winter months, there are few recordings of hedgehogs, as they are hibernating. They'll be back, and when they do footage will appear. In the meantime, check a previous month, or enjoy the mice!

Does more than one hedgehog ever visit? Lots of hedgehogs visit, but they usually come alone. However there have been some recordings of pairs, such as here.

Have baby hedgehogs (hoglets) been? Not yet, but they are more than welcome!

Why are there no videos for certain days? A few reasons really. Perhaps the camera batteries were all discharged, the cameras didn't record, or perhaps there were just no visitors.

How does footage get to the website? The process is part automated, part manual, and continues to evolve. A technical overview can be read here.

Why are the clips of different length? Each of the cameras records for a differing amount of time.

Why does the published clip time not match the overlaid video time? The 'clip' time shown is when the file has been fully created, so should match the final second time, or a few seconds after recording has finished.
