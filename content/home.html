Welcome to Macclesfield Hedgehogs

A video diary of hedgehogs visiting a garden in Macclesfield. 🦔

... and quite often some mice too! 🐁

Sometimes alone, sometimes in groups, always hungry.

Follow their journey through the garden to the 'hedgehog house', where they enjoy food and water.


A great place to start is the Footage page, where you can then discover videos by date.

Further background can be found on the About page.

An overview of the equipment in use can be found on the Cameras page.

Enjoy! 🦔
