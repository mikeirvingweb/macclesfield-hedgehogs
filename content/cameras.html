Cameras

Ceyomur CY95

Dedicated Wildlife Field Camera. Wi-Fi capable.

Solar, Lithium Battery or AA Battery options.

Pros: Excellent image quality and recording options.

Cons: Solar panel is attached, so no use inside.

Access: Automated.

Xbro XC100

An affordable Wi-Fi enabled camera, accessible via a mobile app.

Records to SD Card or Subscription Cloud Service.

Pros: Small. Easily poseable. Good image quality.

Cons: Needs power or battery, batteries don't last long. Flicker prone.

Access: Manual.

Arlo HD

Part of a set of Arlo home security cameras.

Small, and can be mounted to a tripod or magnetic mount.

Pros: Instant alerts.

Cons: Footage kept in the cloud for 7 days only (unless downloaded).

Access: Manual.

Xega RBX-S50

Home Security System Camera. Wi-Fi capable.

Can be moved / rotated by the dedicated mobile app!

Solar or Internal Lithium Battery powered.

Pros: Good image. External Solar Panel.

Cons: Chunky.

Access: Manual.
